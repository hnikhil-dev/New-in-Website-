Auto-Hiding Scrollbar

Scroll down to see the auto-hiding scrollbar in action.

This scrollbar is applied globally to the entire document.

The scrollbar automatically appears when you start scrolling and hides after 2 seconds of inactivity.

Hovering over the page will also reveal the scrollbar.

Try scrolling and waiting to observe the hiding effect.
